Step 1: Add a Task

Task Manager is a digital tool that helps you organize tasks and keep track of what you need to do.It's offering convenience and features like due dates, and the ability to achieve your tasks.

Go to the homepage and fill in the Todo Title and Description. Then click "Add Task" to save it.

Step 2: View Your Todos

All your tasks will be listed below in a table (on desktop) or as expandable cards (on mobile). You can scroll to see them.

Step 3: Attach Your Desire File

Click the Choose file button and choose your desire file and press the attach button to attach your file

Step 4: Update or Delete

Click the Update button to modify a todo. Click Delete to permanently remove it.

Step 5: Mark as Done

Click the checkbox next to each task to mark it as completed. Completed tasks turn Violet with white text.
